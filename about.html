History & Organization

a market leader

in the communications and Information Technology industry

JRM company is a market leader in the communications and Information Technology industry operating in Egypt and Middle East region. JRM was established in 1983, as a small office in Marriot Zamalek as the PBX maintenance company for the hotel. JRM provide comprehensive information technology solutions from design to integration, implementation and support.

Challenges

As a company, JRM must be agile to adapt quickly and appropriately to rapidly changing business environments & new business opportunities, JRM must also deliver new services and capabilities without neglecting the performance or stability of existing ICT solutions and services. Our challenge was to bring communication and technology solutions together and cross-functionally integrate them into a single, scalable model to enable business alignment and a process framework for IT in different verticals.

Solution

Integrated solutions provided by current operating model enables JRM to actively participate in defining these new capabilities, which in turn helps JRM develop better-targeted services to support new business needs.a

Vision

A regional institution leading the market through providing the latest technological solutions with a professional team committed to quality and providing creative solutions.

Operating Model

A business operating model that provides an integrated decision framework for JRM technologists and from different business lines from architectures to operations to translate business needs into specific technology products and services.

Management Direction

JRM management directions is the strategic priorities and business directions for company. following vision and strategy and expressed in high-level business outcomes, as shaping for new solution proposition, serving new markets and delivering new product types.
